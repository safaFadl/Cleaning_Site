House Cleaning Prices Per Square Foot

House cleaning prices per square foot can range from $0.10–$0.17 for regular cleaning, $0.11–$0.22 for deep cleaning, and $0.22–$0.33 for move-out cleaning. The bigger your home and the more cluttered or challenging it is to clean, the more you will be charged per square foot.
